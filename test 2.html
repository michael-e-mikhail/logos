Who We Are?

We are the IEEE SIGHT E-JUST Student Branch Affinity Group: a local branch of the Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology (SIGHT) under the international Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), based at the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST).

We’re a community dedicated to harnessing science, engineering, and technology (STEM solutions) to address pressing humanitarian challenges. Our goal is to build an informed and empowered society by promoting technological awareness and collaborating with like-minded organizations and industries like yours!

Through our upcoming initiative, TechRise Africa, we’re hosting a hackathon themed around humanitarian technology. Our goal is to harness the creativity and passion of young people to develop impactful, technology-driven solutions that benefit communities across Africa and beyond, in sectors including, but not limited to:

  • Agri-Tech
  • Environmental solutions
  • Recycling & Waste management
  • Water Treatment & Conservation
